Privacy Policy

How Walk It collects, uses, and protects your data

What we collect

We collect your username and email from your OAuth provider (e.g., Apple, Google), and your step data.

How we use it

Your step data is used to calculate rewards and show your activity. Account details help operate your account and keep the app secure.

Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties.

Your choices

You can request a data export or deletion at any time by contacting support.

Contact
