Account Deletion

For Walk It • Developer: Cheese Farmer

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to gm@walkitapp.com from the email address associated with the account you want to delete.
  2. Use the subject line: REQUEST FOR ACCOUNT DELETION.
  3. Optionally include any relevant details (e.g., your user ID) to help us locate the account.

What gets deleted

We delete all your data and activity associated with your Walk It account, including your account details (such as email and username) and your step/activity data.

Timeline and retention

We delete all your data and activity within 14 working days of your verified request. No data is kept after deletion.

Confirmation

We will notify you by email once your account has been deleted.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion, contact us at gm@walkitapp.com.
