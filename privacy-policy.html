Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Walk It! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and authentication details.
  • Step & Activity Data: Walking and activity data collected from third-party providers such as Google Fit and Apple Health.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Track and reward your walking activity.
  • Improve and personalize your user experience.
  • Prevent fraudulent activities and maintain security.
  • Send important updates and notifications related to the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following cases:

  • With legal authorities if required by law.
  • With trusted third-party partners, but only when you choose to interact with their services through Walk It.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your personal information. We implement encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your data. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, review, and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your data.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: team@walkitapp.com.
